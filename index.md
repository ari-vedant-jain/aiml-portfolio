## About

My name is Vedant Jain, but I go by **Ari**. I specialize in building **agentic AI systems** for enterprises—from autonomous multi-agent workflows and RAG architectures to AI security, red teaming, and production-grade LLM deployments. Currently Principal AI Solutions Architect at **Protect AI** (acquired by Palo Alto Networks), previously at **AWS** where I led GenAI partner initiatives and contributed to Amazon SageMaker.
